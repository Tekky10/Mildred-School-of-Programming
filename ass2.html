A Girl's Code to Freedom

Once upon a time, there was a beautiful princess who lived in a grand castle. She had everything she could ever want, but she was not happy. She longed for adventure and excitement.

One day, a wicked wizard came to the castle and kidnapped the princess. He took her to his dark and gloomy castle, far away from her home.

The princess was determined to escape. She met a talking cat, a friendly dragon, and a brave knight. Together, they hatched a plan to rescue her.

The cat snuck into the wizard's study and stole his magic wand. The dragon breathed fire on the wizard's castle, creating a distraction. And the knight fought the wizard in single combat.

In the end, the princess was rescued. She thanked her friends for their help, and they all lived happily ever after.
